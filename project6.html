About BirdIQ

BirdIQ is an AI-powered bird classification platform that helps bird enthusiasts, researchers, and nature lovers identify bird species instantly from photos.

Our advanced AI model can recognize hundreds of bird species with high accuracy. Simply upload an image or use your camera, and our AI will analyze the feathers, beak, and patterns to identify the bird.

✨ Click on any discovered bird to see detailed information including habitat, diet, size, lifespan, and fun facts along with YOUR uploaded bird photo!
